Banking & Finance

ISL Accessibility for Banking & Financial Services

Ensuring every deaf customer can access banking, insurance, and financial services independently and with complete dignity - no interpreter required.

The Problem

Deaf Customers Face Real Barriers in Banking

India has over 68 million deaf and hard-of-hearing citizens. Most cannot independently open a bank account, apply for a loan, or complete KYC without relying on a hearing family member to interpret for them.

SignISL eliminates this dependence. With an ISL-enabled tablet at the counter or a mobile banking app with ISL guides, every deaf customer gets the same quality of service as everyone else.

RBI Accessibility Guidelines compliance support

How It Works

Independent Banking in Three Steps

STEP 01

Customer Arrives

  • Deaf customer visits branch
  • Indicates ISL preference
  • Staff activates SignISL tablet
STEP 02

ISL Conversation

  • Teller speaks, ISL avatar signs
  • Customer signs back via camera
  • AI interprets in real time
STEP 03

Transaction Complete

  • Process completed independently
  • No interpreter needed
  • Full audit trail maintained
