Ensuring deaf individuals have full, equal access to justice - in courtrooms, police stations, and legal consultations across India.

Equal Access to Justice

Every Deaf Individual Deserves to Understand Every Word

In courtrooms, police stations, and legal consultations across India, deaf individuals are routinely denied equal access to proceedings because no ISL interpreter is available. This is a fundamental denial of justice.

SignISL places an ISL avatar screen beside the witness box, at the police counter, or on a lawyer's tablet - so every spoken word is simultaneously signed in ISL, in real time, without any human interpreter needed.

Judiciary Accessibility Mandate

The RPWD Act 2016 requires all public authorities to provide ISL interpretation for deaf persons in official proceedings. SignISL provides a scalable, always-available solution.
