Our Story

A Step Towards Accessibility 

SignISL was formally incorporated in May 2025 as Jeevyam Technologies - a 50/50 partnership between founders Priyam Arora and Sanjeev Sharma. By September 2025, our first working prototype was live: a full speech-to-gloss-to-avatar pipeline built in Python, deployed on a VPS, signing in real time.

In December 2025 we won 2nd place at IIM Mumbai Ideathon (₹50,000 prize) out of 200+ teams. In January 2026 we filed Provisional Patent #202611005957 protecting our translation pipeline. February 2026 saw us win EIS 4.0 (₹25,000). By March 2026 we had opened an API integration pathway with Digital India's national language platform - and are actively deploying across Indian Railways, Delhi Metro, and Indira Gandhi Airport.
