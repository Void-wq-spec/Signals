Integration

One Line. That's All.

Paste this into your website's HTML. The ISL widget appears instantly - no configuration required.

<!-- Add SignISL ISL widget to any website -->
<script src="https://cdn.signisl.in/widget.js"
        data-key="YOUR_API_KEY"
        data-lang="hi,en,ta"
        data-position="bottom-right"></script>
