How It Works

From Voice to Sign in Under 200ms

A three-stage AI pipeline converts spoken language directly to a signing ISL avatar - no intermediary or typing needed.

STEP 01

Voice Input

  • Continuous microphone listening
  • 12+ Indian languages detected
  • Noise-filtered in real time
STEP 02

AI Processing

  • Speech-to-text transcription
  • ISL gloss generation via NLP
  • Grammar restructured for ISL syntax
STEP 03

ISL Avatar

  • Animated avatar signs in real time
  • Adjustable signing speed
  • Caption displayed simultaneously
