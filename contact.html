Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How long does it take to integrate the widget? +
The SignISL website widget takes under 5 minutes to add - paste one line of JavaScript into your HTML. For kiosk deployments or API integrations, our team provides full technical support and onboarding.
Which Indian languages are supported? +
We currently support Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, Malayalam, and Urdu - 12+ languages in total, with more being added continuously.
Is SignISL compliant with the RPWD Act 2016? +
Yes. SignISL is designed to help government bodies and public institutions meet their obligations under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016, making public communications accessible to India's deaf community.
Is there a free pilot for government bodies? +
Yes - government bodies, schools, hospitals, and NGOs can access a 30-day free pilot deployment. Contact our founders directly to discuss the scope and terms of the pilot programme.
Do you offer discounts for schools and NGOs? +
Yes - we offer significant discounts for schools, government bodies, and registered NGOs. Contact us with your organisation details and we will work out a plan that fits your budget and scale.
