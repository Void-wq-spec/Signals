Every Live Moment, Accessible

Live Translation goes beyond pre-recorded content. It translates any live audio source - a teacher, a minister, a doctor, or a news anchor - into ISL in real time, so deaf viewers never miss a single moment.

From government press conferences to school assemblies, from hospital announcements to live TV broadcasts, SignISL's Live Translation engine brings every live moment within reach of India's 68 million deaf and hard-of-hearing citizens.
